HOW TO PLAY

The puck will drop after the countdown.

Hit the puck 7 times in your opponent Uni's goal to win.

Slide your finger left and right across your phone screen to block Uni from hitting the puck into your goal line.

Discover Coach holiday gifts at the end of the game.
