HOW TO PLAY

Collect as many Coach holiday gifts as you can in 1 minute.

Swipe left or right to make your car change directions.

If you crash your car, you end the game. If you don't crash the car in 1 minute, you win.

Discover Coach holiday gifts at the end of the game.
